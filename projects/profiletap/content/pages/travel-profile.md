# Travel QR Code Profile — Smart Safety for Every Journey

Lost luggage. An airport emergency with a low phone battery. An international trip where nobody knows how to reach your family back home. Travel exposes the gaps in how we manage our identity and contact information. A ProfileTap travel profile closes those gaps — before you need them.

---

## Your Travel Identity, Always Ready

Most people have experienced the anxiety of lost luggage. You land in Dubai or London or Singapore, walk to the carousel, and your bag is not there. The airline asks for your contact details while you are overseas. Your Indian SIM may not be active. The number written on your luggage tag is your old number from three years ago.

A travel QR profile solves this from every angle. Your luggage tag carries a QR code that links to a live profile. That profile shows your name, your contact number, your destination, your hotel, your emergency contact back home, and any other information you choose to include. Anyone who finds your bag — an airline ground crew member, another traveller, a hotel porter — scans the tag and has everything they need to get your luggage back to you.

And if your contact details ever change? Update the profile once. Every QR code that links to it automatically reflects the new information.

## Three Ways Travellers Use ProfileTap

**Luggage tags that work even when you are offline**
Attach a QR tag to every piece of luggage before your trip. If a bag goes missing, the airline or finder scans the tag and reaches you through call privacy — they can call you without ever seeing your personal phone number. Even if you have changed SIMs or are roaming on a different network, the profile itself is always accessible.

**An emergency contact card in your wallet**
Print a small QR code and keep it in your wallet behind your ID. If you are in an accident or medical emergency away from home — a road trip from Bangalore to Coorg, a trek in Himachal, a solo trip through Rajasthan — first responders or hospital staff scan the card and immediately have your blood type, medical conditions, ICE (In Case of Emergency) contacts, and travel insurance details.

**A contact card for international travel**
When you are abroad and need to give someone a way to reach you — a local contact, a tour operator, a conference host — your ProfileTap travel profile gives them everything in a single scan. No fumbling for a local SIM card number, no sending WhatsApp messages at 2am to update everyone on your new temporary number. One scan covers it all.

## Privacy While You Travel

Putting your phone number on a luggage tag feels like a necessary risk. But it is not one you have to take with ProfileTap.

Call privacy means the contact number on your travel profile routes through ProfileTap's masking layer. Someone who finds your bag and calls the number reaches you on your real phone — but never sees your actual number. This is particularly useful for solo travellers, especially women travelling alone, who want to be reachable in an emergency without broadcasting their personal mobile number on every piece of luggage they check in.

WhatsApp privacy works the same way. A local contact you meet on a trip can WhatsApp you through your travel profile without you sharing your personal number at all. You decide later whether to connect directly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is a travel QR profile?**
A travel QR profile is a digital profile accessible through a QR code that holds your travel contact information, emergency contacts, medical details, and any other information relevant to your journey. You attach the QR code to your luggage, carry it in your wallet, or share it with contacts you meet while travelling. It is always live and always up to date.

**How does it help if my luggage gets lost?**
Your luggage QR tag links to a live profile with your name, contact number (masked through call privacy), destination, and hotel details. Airline ground staff, airport personnel, or anyone who finds the bag scans the tag and immediately knows who owns the luggage and how to reach you — even if you are already at your destination without your bag.

**Is my personal data private?**
You control exactly what information appears on your travel profile. Call privacy and WhatsApp privacy mean your personal phone numbers are never directly visible to strangers — they can reach you through ProfileTap's masking system without ever seeing your real number. You can also set the profile to show only the minimum information necessary.

**How do I attach it to my luggage?**
Download and print the QR code from your ProfileTap dashboard. You can print it on a self-laminating luggage tag (available at most stationery stores), slip it into a clear tag sleeve on your bag, or use a waterproof sticker. The QR code never changes, so you can use the same tag for every trip — just update the profile before you leave.

**What does a travel profile cost?**

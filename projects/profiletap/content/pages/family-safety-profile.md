# Family Safety Profiles — QR Emergency Contact for Every Family Member

When something goes wrong, seconds matter. A family safety profile puts the right contact information in front of the right person — instantly, with a single scan — without compromising the privacy of your family's personal details.

---

## What is a Family Safety Profile?

A family safety profile is a digital profile accessible through a QR code that carries emergency contact details and relevant information for a specific family member. When someone scans the QR code — a teacher, a passerby, a hospital staff member — they see exactly what they need to reach you and help the person in front of them.

For a child, that might be a parent's contact details, the child's school name, and any medical conditions or allergies. For an elderly parent, it might be their doctor's number, their blood type, their medications, and the emergency contact for their primary caregiver. For a family member with special needs, it might include specific instructions for how to communicate and who to call.

The profile is always live. Update the information once and every QR code that links to that profile shows the new details immediately.

## Privacy Built In

A safety profile solves one problem while potentially creating another: you need strangers to be able to contact you, but you do not necessarily want to broadcast your personal phone number on a tag attached to a child's bag.

ProfileTap handles this with call privacy and WhatsApp privacy. When someone calls the number shown on your safety profile, the call routes through ProfileTap's masking layer. You receive it on your real phone. The caller gets through to you. Neither party sees the other's personal number unless you choose to share it.

This is especially important for children's safety tags and elderly parent profiles, where the emergency contact is typically a parent or adult child who may not want their personal mobile number on a physical tag that could be seen by anyone.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What information should go on a family safety profile?**
The essentials are: the family member's name, at least one emergency contact number, and any critical medical information (blood type, allergies, medications, conditions). For children, adding the school name and a secondary contact is helpful. For elderly family members, include the doctor's number and a list of current medications. You control exactly what is visible — start with what is genuinely necessary in an emergency.

**Is it safe to show personal information on a QR code?**
ProfileTap safety profiles are designed with this concern in mind. You choose what information is visible. Call privacy and WhatsApp privacy ensure that phone numbers on the profile are masked — scanners can reach you without ever seeing your real number. Profile analytics let you monitor how often the profile is being scanned, so unusual activity is visible.

**Can a stranger access the full profile without scanning the QR code?**
A safety profile's QR code links to a specific profile URL. Someone who does not have the QR code or the direct URL cannot find the profile through ProfileTap's platform — it is not a publicly listed directory. The profile is only accessible to people who have the QR code or the link, which in practice means someone physically present with the tag.

**How do I set up a child safety tag?**
Create a ProfileTap account, add a new profile for your child under the family safety type, fill in the relevant details (name, parent contact, school, medical notes), enable call privacy so your real number is masked, download the QR code, and print or order a physical tag. The entire process takes under ten minutes. You can update the profile at any time and the QR code will always reflect the latest information.

**What does a family safety profile cost?**

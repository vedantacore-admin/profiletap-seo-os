## Why Doctors Need a Digital Business Card

Indian healthcare is relationship-driven. A patient who trusts their doctor refers family members, leaves reviews, and returns. The problem is that most clinics make it unnecessarily hard to stay connected after the appointment ends.

**Your personal number is exposed.** When you print your mobile number on a paper card, it goes to every patient you have ever seen. Late-night calls, WhatsApp messages from strangers, requests for free advice from distant acquaintances — these erode the boundary between your professional and personal life. There is no off switch once that number is out.

**Paper cards disappear.** A patient scans for a specialist referral three months after their last visit. They remember your name but cannot find your card. They search online, find a competitor, and your referral opportunity is gone. Paper cards are lost, discarded, or simply never saved.

**Reviews require effort you do not have.** Google reviews are increasingly how patients in Mumbai, Chennai, Bangalore, and across India choose a doctor. Asking a patient to leave a review after a consultation feels awkward. Writing a response to every review takes time you do not have. Most clinics collect reviews by accident rather than by design, and their online ratings drift.

**Referral management is opaque.** If you refer patients to a specialist or a diagnostic centre, there is often no easy way to share the right contact details quickly. You write a number on a prescription slip or dictate it verbally. It gets lost.

ProfileTap changes all of this through a single, tap-enabled smart profile that your reception team can share on your behalf.

## How ProfileTap Works for Doctors

### One tap at reception

Your ProfileTap NFC card sits at the reception desk. At the end of every appointment, the receptionist taps it on the patient's phone. The patient's browser opens your ProfileTap profile instantly — no app download required. They see your name, your specialisation, your clinic address, contact links, and a direct link to leave you a Google review. They save it in one tap.

The same NFC card can be mounted as a small standee at the reception counter so patients can self-serve while they wait.

### QR code for every patient touchpoint

Every ProfileTap profile includes a unique QR code. Print it on your prescription pad headers, display it on the clinic wall, add it to your visiting card design as a secondary element, or embed it in your clinic's WhatsApp business message. When a patient scans it, they land on your live profile — which is always current, even if you change your clinic address or phone number.

### Call and WhatsApp privacy

With ProfileTap's call masking and WhatsApp masking, patients can reach you through the platform without ever seeing your personal mobile number. You receive the call or message through a privacy layer. You maintain availability without sacrificing personal boundaries. When you are off duty, the privacy layer is your first line of protection.

### AI-assisted review collection

ProfileTap's AI review assist feature helps your clinic grow its Google review count systematically. Your profile includes a direct review prompt. When patients visit your profile after their appointment, the path to leaving a review is one click. For reviews you have already received, AI review assist suggests professional, personalised response drafts in seconds — no more staring at a negative review unsure of what to say.

### Profile analytics

ProfileTap tracks how many patients viewed your profile, which links they clicked, and when traffic spikes occur. If you run a specialist clinic, this data tells you which referral channels are sending you patients and which touchpoints your marketing budget is actually reaching.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does ProfileTap work without patients downloading an app?**
Yes. Patients tap your NFC card or scan your QR code and the profile opens directly in their phone's browser. There is nothing to download. The profile works on every smartphone — iPhone and Android.

**How does call masking protect my personal number?**
When call masking is enabled, your ProfileTap profile shows a masked contact link rather than your personal mobile number. Patients initiate the call through the platform. You receive the call on your phone, but the patient never sees your direct number. You can disable or limit access to the masking feature when you are off duty.

**Can my receptionist manage the profile without knowing my login?**
Yes. The manage account collaborators feature lets you add your receptionist or practice manager as a collaborator. They can update the profile, respond to reviews, and share your card — all from their own login. You remain the account owner and can remove collaborators at any time.

**Is ProfileTap compatible with Google reviews for Indian clinics?**
Yes. ProfileTap's AI review assist is designed to work with Google reviews. Your profile includes a direct link to your Google My Business review page. The AI-generated response drafts work with Google's review format, and the system is tested with Indian clinic and hospital review patterns.

**What if I move clinics or change my contact number?**
Update your ProfileTap profile once and the change is reflected everywhere your profile has been shared — including every NFC tap and QR scan that led a patient to your profile in the past. You do not need to reprint cards or redistribute contact details. Every patient who has previously viewed your profile will see the new information the next time they visit the link.

# Google Review Card NFC — Collect More Reviews with a Single Tap

Google reviews drive decisions. When a patient in Chennai searches for a dentist, when someone in Bangalore looks for a salon, when a family in Mumbai picks a restaurant — they check the reviews first. The businesses that collect reviews consistently win. A ProfileTap Google review card makes collecting reviews as easy as handing someone a card and asking them to tap.

## How Google Review Cards Work

Each ProfileTap review card has an embedded NFC chip and a printed QR code. When a customer taps the card on their phone or scans the QR code, they are taken directly to your Google review page — or to a ProfileTap review flow that guides them through the process.

The review flow is where ProfileTap stands apart from generic NFC cards. Connected to [ProfileTap's Business AI tools](/business-ai), the card can trigger an AI-assisted review prompt that helps your customer write a specific, helpful review. Instead of a blank text box that most people abandon, they see guided prompts like "What did you like about your visit?" and "Would you recommend this to others?" The result is more completed reviews and higher-quality feedback.

This is not about manufacturing fake reviews. It is about reducing friction for real customers who had a good experience but would not bother writing about it unprompted.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do review cards only work with Google reviews?

ProfileTap review cards are optimised for Google reviews because Google is the primary discovery platform for local businesses in India. However, you can configure your review flow to link to other platforms — Practo for doctors, Zomato for restaurants, or any review platform that accepts a direct link. The AI-assisted prompts work regardless of the destination platform.

### How does the AI review prompt work?

When a customer taps the card, they can be directed to a simple review flow powered by [ProfileTap Business AI](/business-ai). Instead of facing a blank review box, they see guided questions about their experience. The AI helps them structure their thoughts into a clear, genuine review. The customer always has full control over what they submit — the AI assists, it does not write for them.

### Can I track how many reviews I collect from the cards?

Yes. ProfileTap analytics track every tap and scan on your review cards. You can see how many customers engaged, how many started the review flow, and correlate that with your actual Google review count over time. This data helps you understand which locations, time periods, or card placements generate the most reviews.

### How many review cards should I order?

It depends on your business volume. A clinic seeing 30 patients per day might keep 2-3 cards at reception and one standee format on the counter. A restaurant might want a standee card on every table. Start with a small order, measure your review collection rate, and scale up. Bulk pricing is available for orders of 20 or more.

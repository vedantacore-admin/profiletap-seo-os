# NFC Keychain — Share Your Profile Anywhere You Go

A business card lives in your wallet. A standee stays on your desk. But an NFC keychain goes everywhere — clipped to your keys, hooked to your bag, or attached to a lanyard. When a networking opportunity comes up unexpectedly, you are ready. Tap and share. That is the idea behind the ProfileTap NFC keychain.

## How NFC Keychains Work

Each ProfileTap keychain has an embedded NFC chip. Hold it against someone's smartphone and your digital profile opens instantly in their browser — no app required. The keychain also has a scannable QR code for phones that are not NFC-enabled.

Like every ProfileTap product, the keychain connects to your digital profile. Update your details online, and the keychain reflects the change immediately. There is nothing to reprogram or replace.

The keychain is compact enough that carrying it adds zero friction to your day. Clip it to your car keys, your office lanyard, your laptop bag, or your gym bag. When the moment comes — a conversation at a coffee shop, a chance meeting at an airport, a parent at your child's school — your profile is one tap away.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can one keychain be used for both business and safety purposes?

Each keychain links to one ProfileTap profile at a time. If you want to use it for business networking, link it to your business profile. If you want to use it for family safety, link it to a safety profile. You can change the linked profile at any time through your ProfileTap dashboard — so one keychain can serve different purposes at different times, just not simultaneously.

### Is the keychain waterproof?

ProfileTap NFC keychains are water-resistant and built for everyday conditions — rain, sweat, accidental splashes. They are not designed for prolonged submersion, but they will handle anything a normal day throws at them. The NFC chip is sealed inside the casing.

### How is the keychain different from a business card?

Functionally, the NFC technology is the same — both tap to share your digital profile. The difference is form factor and carry convenience. A business card lives in your wallet or card case. A keychain attaches to something you already carry every day. It also serves safety use cases that a card does not — you would not clip a business card to a child's school bag.

### Can I use the keychain for emergency contact information?

Yes. Link the keychain to a [family safety profile](/family-safety-profile) with emergency contacts, medical information, and family details. If someone taps the keychain, they see the safety profile and can contact you through ProfileTap's masked calling — your real phone number stays private.

## What a Digital Profile Does

A digital profile replaces the scattered collection of links, cards, and contact methods that most people juggle daily. Instead of handing someone a paper visiting card, texting them your LinkedIn, emailing them your portfolio link, and hoping they save your phone number correctly — you share one link. That link opens your ProfileTap profile, which contains everything the other person needs in one place.

Your profile is live. You update it once and every person who visits the link from that point forward sees the current version. Change your phone number, add a new social account, update your job title — it reflects immediately. No reprinting. No resending.

This is not a website. It is not a social media page. It is a purpose-built identity page designed to make sharing your professional or personal information fast, clean, and current.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is the free digital profile really free — permanently?
Yes. The free tier has no time limit, no trial period, and no credit card requirement. You get a complete digital profile with QR sharing, basic themes, and unlimited updates. The free tier exists because ProfileTap believes everyone should have access to a basic digital identity. Pro features are available when you need them, but the free profile does not expire.

### What is the difference between a digital profile and a website?
A digital profile is a single, structured page designed for sharing your identity and contact information. It is not a multi-page website, a blog, or a CMS. It loads instantly, looks clean on mobile, and is built specifically for the use case of "someone needs to know who I am and how to reach me." If you need a full website, you need a website builder. If you need a fast, professional way to share your information, a digital profile is the right tool.

### Can I use my digital profile as a link-in-bio?
Yes. Many ProfileTap users set their profile link as their link-in-bio on Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, and other platforms. Your profile page shows all your links in one place — social accounts, portfolio, newsletter, shop, or anything else. Pro users get analytics to see which links are being clicked and by how many people.

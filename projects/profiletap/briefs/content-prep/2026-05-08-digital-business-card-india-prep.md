## Notes for writer
- Use "digital visiting card" as a secondary term in the copy (2–3 mentions) — it is the colloquial Indian term and captures an additional keyword cluster
- Always use "crore" not "million" for Indian audience (e.g., "10 crore Indian professionals" not "100 million Indian professionals")
- Name WhatsApp masking explicitly — this is a differentiator and a search behaviour trigger for privacy-conscious Indian professionals
- Reference UPI QR adoption as context for why QR is the natural sharing method — this is a uniquely Indian cultural reference that no global competitor page includes
- Mention INR pricing — do not use USD pricing comparisons; the audience is India-first
- Avoid using "visiting card" as the H1 or primary framing — it is a secondary keyword only; "digital business card" is the primary anchor
- Do not use the phrase "NFC card company" — always frame ProfileTap as a "smart identity management platform"
- The page should feel like it was written by someone who understands the India professional market — not a translated global product page

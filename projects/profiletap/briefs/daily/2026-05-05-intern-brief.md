In 2026, if you're still handing out paper business cards at networking events,
you're missing something fundamental: how we network in India has changed,
but our tools haven't kept up.

## The Problem With Paper — and First-Gen Digital Cards

Walk into any startup event in Bangalore or Mumbai, and you'll see the same
pattern: founders exchange paper cards, immediately forget who they met, and
follow up on WhatsApp a week later with "Hi, we met at [event], here's my
number." The entire process leaks context.

First-gen digital business cards tried to fix the paper problem — tap an NFC
card, open a contact page, save the number. That works. But it solves only
one slice of the identity problem.

## Identity Isn't One Thing — Especially in India

Consider what a single person actually needs today:

- A **business identity** for networking, client meetings, and B2B relationships
- A **creator identity** for Instagram collabs, brand partnerships, and audience growth
- A **vehicle identity** — a QR sticker on your car or bike so someone can
  reach you without knowing your personal number
- A **pet ID** — a scannable tag on your dog's collar showing vet details
  and owner contacts if they get lost
- A **family safety profile** — for kids at school events, senior citizens,
  or your luggage when travelling

These are not niche use cases. They happen to the same person. Yet every
existing tool forces you to manage them separately — or not at all.

## What Smart Identity Management Looks Like

ProfileTap is building a smart identity management platform — one system
that covers all these contexts. Core capabilities:

- **NFC + QR sharing**: tap or scan to share your profile instantly, no app
  install required on the recipient's side
- **Multi-profile types**: switch between your business, creator, vehicle,
  or family safety profile on the same account
- **AI review assist**: automated tools to help Indian businesses manage
  Google reviews and online reputation
- **Privacy-native design**: call masking and WhatsApp masking so you share
  contact access without exposing your personal number — critical for Indian
  professional norms where sharing your mobile in every business context
  creates real privacy risk
- **Team management**: multi-account support for business owners managing
  a team, front desk, or multiple locations

## Why This Matters for the Indian Market

India's networking culture is uniquely suited for smart identity tools:

- We're QR-native — we scan for UPI payments, restaurant menus, event check-ins
- We're WhatsApp-first — professional follow-ups happen on WhatsApp more than email
- Privacy matters differently here — most Indian professionals do not want
  their personal mobile number exposed in every business interaction

ProfileTap is built around these realities: INR pricing, India-specific use
cases, physical NFC and QR products delivered across India.

## The Opportunity

India has 63 million registered businesses and 10+ crore working professionals.
Almost none have a coherent digital identity system.

If you're building or advising startups in India, this is a category worth
watching — smart identity infrastructure is becoming table stakes for any
professional who wants to operate credibly in a digital-first economy.

Curious what the platform looks like:

## India Media Landscape Context

### Key Publication Tiers

Tier 1 (DA 70+, high editorial standards):
- yourstory.com, entrepreneur.com, businessworld.in, lonelyplanet.com
- Approach: founder story, data-backed insights, expert contribution
- Lead time: 2-4 weeks for editorial review
- Content quality: original research, unique data, founder narrative

Tier 2 (DA 50-70, strong niche authority):
- inc42.com, entrackr.com, exchange4media.com, socialsamosa.com, carwale.com, tripoto.com
- Approach: product story, category insight, comparison roundup contribution
- Lead time: 1-3 weeks
- Content quality: industry insight, practical tools comparison

Tier 3 (DA 30-50, niche or community):
- indianstartupnews.com, shoutmeloud.com, petindia.com
- Approach: guest post, tool review, roundup inclusion
- Lead time: 1-2 weeks
- Content quality: practical, how-to, tool comparison

### Editorial Standards by Type
- Startup media: wants founder narrative, market data, growth story
- Business media: wants enterprise value prop, ROI angle, case studies
- Marketing media: wants creator economy data, campaign examples
- Niche blogs: wants practical tool reviews, comparison tables, how-to guides

## Anchor Text Rules

### Anchor Type Distribution Targets
- `brand`: 30-40% (e.g., "ProfileTap")
- `partial`: 30-40% (e.g., "smart identity profile for creators", "digital business card platform")
- `exact`: 15-25% (e.g., "digital business card india", "hihello alternative india")
- `generic`: 5-10% (e.g., "learn more", "visit here")

### Page-Specific Anchor Guidelines

| Target Page | Preferred Exact Anchor | Preferred Partial Anchors |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `/` | ProfileTap | smart identity platform, identity management platform |
| `/digital-business-card-india` | digital business card india | digital business card for Indian businesses |
| `/nfc-business-card-india` | nfc business card india | NFC-enabled business profiles |
| `/digital-business-card-for-creators` | digital business card for creators | smart identity profile for creators |
| `/hihello-alternative-india` | hihello alternative india | HiHello alternative for India |
| `/digital-business-card-for-doctors` | digital business card for doctors | digital networking for doctors |
| `/linktree-alternative-for-creators` | linktree alternative for creators | link-in-bio alternative for Indian creators |

### Rules
- Never use the same exact anchor text for more than 25% of links to a single page
- Vary anchors naturally across publications
- India-targeted pages should have some anchors with "India" modifier
- Brand anchors should always be welcome and safe
- Avoid over-optimized exact match anchors on Tier 1 publications

## Prospect Qualification Criteria

### Minimum Requirements
- Domain Authority (DA): 30+ for any prospect
- Relevance: publication must cover topics related to the target page's hub
- Market: Indian publications preferred for India-targeted pages; global OK for IN+GLOBAL pages
- Editorial quality: must have original editorial content, not just directories or link farms
- Active publication: content published within the last 3 months

### Scoring Matrix

| Factor | Weight | Scoring |
| --- | --- | --- |
| DA | 30% | 30-49: 1pt, 50-69: 2pt, 70+: 3pt |
| Hub relevance | 25% | Tangential: 1pt, Related: 2pt, Exact: 3pt |
| India market fit | 20% | Global only: 1pt, India section: 2pt, India-focused: 3pt |
| Editorial quality | 15% | User-generated: 1pt, Mixed: 2pt, Professional editorial: 3pt |
| Link placement | 10% | Sidebar/footer: 1pt, In-content: 2pt, Feature/interview: 3pt |

Minimum qualifying score: 8/15 points

## CSV Tracking Workflow

### Step 1: Prospect (backlink_targets.csv)
Add qualified prospects with:
- `domain`, `site_type`, `target_page_slug`, `target_keyword`
- `suggested_anchor`, `anchor_type`, `target_market`
- `link_angle` (from the outreach angle library above)
- `priority` (P1 for Tier 1-2 publications, P2 for Tier 3)
- `status`: starts as `prospecting`

### Step 2: Outreach (outreach_tracker.csv)
Move to outreach tracker when ready to contact:
- `domain`, `target_page_slug`, `contact_name`, `contact_email`
- `status`: `not_started` -> `contacted` -> `follow_up_1` -> `follow_up_2` -> `won` / `lost` / `no_response`
- `first_contact_date`, `last_contact_date`, `next_follow_up_date`
- `outcome`: result summary
- `live_url`: published link URL when won

### Step 3: Follow-up Cadence
- First contact: initial pitch email
- Follow-up 1: 5-7 days after first contact
- Follow-up 2: 10-14 days after first contact
- Close as `no_response` after 21 days with no reply
- Never send more than 3 outreach emails to the same contact

## Outreach Email Templates

### Template 1: Founder Story (for startup media)
Subject: How ProfileTap is rethinking identity management in India
Body: Brief founder narrative, India market insight, offer for interview or feature. Include 1-2 data points about digital business card adoption in India. Link to ProfileTap homepage.

### Template 2: Data-Backed Category (for business media)
Subject: Digital business cards in India -- growth data and trends
Body: Lead with a market data point. Offer original data or expert comment. Position ProfileTap as a source, not the story. Suggest contextual mention in upcoming category coverage.

### Template 3: Comparison Roundup (for review blogs)
Subject: Include ProfileTap in your {competitor} alternatives roundup
Body: Brief ProfileTap positioning. 3-4 bullet differentiators. Offer a free account for review. Suggest anchor text and target page.

### Template 4: Guest Post (for niche blogs)
Subject: Guest post pitch: {topic relevant to their audience}
Body: Propose 2-3 topic options relevant to the publication's audience. Offer original content with one contextual backlink. Reference their recent coverage for relevance.

### Template 5: Expert Contribution (for media roundups)
Subject: Expert comment available: {topic}
Body: Offer a ready-to-publish expert quote on a trending topic. Include the quote directly in the email. Request attribution with a link to the relevant ProfileTap page.

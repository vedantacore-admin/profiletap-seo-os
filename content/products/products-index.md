# ProfileTap Products — Smart Identity for Every Part of Your Life

ProfileTap is more than a digital business card. It is a smart identity platform with physical and digital products designed to help you share who you are — professionally, personally, and in emergencies — with a single tap or scan.

Every ProfileTap product connects to your digital profile. Update your details once, and every card, tag, sticker, and standee linked to your account reflects the change instantly. No reprinting. No wasted inventory.

---

## Business Products

Products built for Indian professionals, teams, and businesses that need to share their identity and collect reviews efficiently.

**[Metal Business Cards](/products/metal-business-cards)** — Premium NFC-enabled cards in black, silver, and gold. Tap to share your digital profile instantly. The flagship ProfileTap product for professionals who want to make a lasting impression at networking events, client meetings, and trade expos.

**[PVC Business Cards](/products/pvc-business-cards)** — Durable NFC and QR cards in white and black. The same smart sharing technology as the metal cards at a more accessible price point. Ideal for teams ordering in bulk.

**[Business Standees](/products/business-standees)** — NFC and QR-enabled standees for reception desks, countertops, and event tables. Customers tap or scan to view your profile and leave Google reviews. Multiple design options available.

**[Review Cards](/products/review-cards)** — NFC and QR cards that direct customers straight to your Google review page. Paired with AI-assisted review prompts to help businesses collect more reviews with less effort.

**[Keychains](/products/keychains)** — Compact NFC keychains for professionals on the move. Attach to your lanyard, bag, or keys and share your profile anywhere. Also works as a safety tag for family members.

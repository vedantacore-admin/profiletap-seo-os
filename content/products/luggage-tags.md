## How Smart Luggage Tags Work

The process is designed for the real-world scenario where it matters most: someone has your bag and you do not have theirs.

**Step 1 — Attach the tag to your luggage.**
The tag loops onto any suitcase handle, backpack strap, or travel bag. It sits alongside airline tags without interfering.

**Step 2 — Someone finds your bag and scans the QR code.**
An airline employee, a hotel concierge, a fellow passenger, or an honest stranger at a railway station points their phone camera at the QR code on your tag. No app required.

**Step 3 — Your travel profile opens.**
They see your name, a contact option, and any notes you have added — your flight number, hotel name, or a message like "if found, please contact the nearest airline desk."

**Step 4 — They reach you through call masking.**
The finder taps the call button. The call routes through ProfileTap's masking system. They reach you on your real phone. You coordinate the return of your luggage. Neither party's personal number is ever shared.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the finder need to download an app to scan the tag?
No. Any modern smartphone camera can scan a QR code natively. The finder points their camera at the tag, taps the notification, and your travel profile opens in their browser. No app, no account, no friction.

### Can I update my profile details between trips?
Yes. The QR code on your tag always links to the same profile URL. Before each trip, update your profile with your current flight number, hotel, or travel dates. The next scan reflects whatever information is currently on the profile. You never need to buy a new tag.

### How does call masking protect my number?
When the finder taps the call button on your travel profile, the call routes through ProfileTap's masking system. They hear it ring, you answer on your real phone, and you coordinate the luggage return. At no point does the finder see your personal mobile number, and you do not see theirs. Once the interaction is over, there is no record of your actual number on their device.

### Is the tag durable enough for checked luggage?
All ProfileTap luggage tags are designed for the reality of checked baggage — conveyor belts, cargo holds, weather exposure, and rough handling. The fixed and custom tags are made from reinforced plastic with a secure loop. The premium leather tag uses a buckle strap rated for the weight and stress of being pulled through airport systems. The QR code itself includes error correction, so minor scuffs will not prevent scanning.

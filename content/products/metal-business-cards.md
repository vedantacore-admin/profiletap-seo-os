## How Metal NFC Business Cards Work

Each ProfileTap metal business card has an embedded NFC chip and a printed QR code on the back. When someone taps the card against their smartphone — or scans the QR code with their camera — your digital profile opens instantly in their browser. No app required on the recipient's side.

Your digital profile includes your name, designation, company, phone number, email, social links, portfolio, and any other details you choose to display. Update your profile online, and every card linked to your account reflects the change immediately. No reprinting, no stickers over old phone numbers.

The NFC chip works with all modern iPhones (iPhone 7 and later) and most Android phones manufactured after 2018. The QR code serves as a fallback for older devices.

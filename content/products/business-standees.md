# NFC Business Standee — Collect Reviews and Share Your Profile from Any Counter

Your reception desk, checkout counter, or event table is prime real estate. Every customer who stands there is a potential review, a saved contact, and a future repeat visit. A ProfileTap NFC business standee turns that moment into action — one tap or scan and your customer is viewing your profile, saving your details, or leaving a Google review.

## How the NFC Business Standee Works

Each ProfileTap standee has an embedded NFC chip and a printed QR code. Place it on your counter, reception desk, or table. When a customer taps their phone on the standee — or scans the QR code — your ProfileTap profile opens in their browser. From there, they can save your contact details, browse your services, or leave a Google review.

The standee connects to your ProfileTap digital profile, so any update you make online is reflected instantly. Change your operating hours, add a new service, or update your review link — no need to replace the standee.

For businesses using [ProfileTap's AI review tools](/business-ai), the standee can direct customers to a review flow that uses AI-assisted prompts to help them write helpful, detailed reviews. This is not about faking reviews — it is about making it easy for genuinely satisfied customers to say what they liked.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many reviews can I realistically collect with a standee?

Results vary by business type and foot traffic, but businesses using ProfileTap standees consistently report a steady increase in Google reviews within the first month. The key is placement — put the standee where customers naturally pause (reception desk, billing counter, table) and the tap-to-review flow does the rest.

### Can I link the standee to my Google review page directly?

Yes. When you set up your ProfileTap profile, you add your Google Business review link. Customers who tap or scan the standee are guided directly to your Google review page. You can also configure the flow to show your [business profile](/business-identity) first, with the review prompt as a follow-up action.

### Does the standee need batteries or charging?

No. ProfileTap standees are passive NFC devices — they draw power from the customer's phone during the tap. The QR code requires no power at all. There is nothing to charge, no battery to replace, and no Wi-Fi connection needed for the standee itself.

### Can I use standees at multiple locations?

Yes. Each standee links to a ProfileTap profile, and you can have separate profiles for each location. If you run a chain of clinics or restaurants, each location gets its own standee linked to its own profile with location-specific details and review links. Manage all locations from one dashboard using ProfileTap's multi-account management.

## Why QR Stickers Beat the Dashboard Phone Number

The current system in most Indian cities is informal and flawed. You leave your phone number on a paper slip on the dashboard. That slip blows away, gets soaked in rain, or fades in the sun. Worse, your personal mobile number is visible to every person who walks past your car — delivery agents, salespeople, strangers with no connection to any parking issue.

A ProfileTap QR sticker replaces that system entirely. The sticker is permanent, weatherproof, and links to a digital profile with a masked contact option. The person who needs you to move scans the sticker, taps call, and reaches you on your phone. Your real number is never displayed, never stored, and never exposed.

## Call Masking: The Core Selling Point

This is the feature that makes ProfileTap vehicle stickers fundamentally different from writing your number on a piece of paper.

When someone scans your QR sticker, they see your vehicle profile with a contact button. They tap it. The call routes through ProfileTap's call masking system. Here is what happens:

- **They hear it ring.** You answer on your real phone.
- **You talk.** You sort out the parking issue, the emergency, or whatever prompted the scan.
- **Nobody's number is exposed.** The caller does not see your mobile number. You do not see theirs. The interaction is private on both sides.

This matters for several reasons. Women riders and drivers no longer need to choose between being reachable and being private. Night-shift workers do not get woken by random calls from people who noted their number weeks ago. And nobody's number ends up in a spam database because it was visible on a dashboard for months.

WhatsApp masking works the same way. If the person prefers to message rather than call, they can reach you on WhatsApp without accessing your personal WhatsApp number.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will the sticker survive monsoon rain and summer heat?
Yes. ProfileTap vehicle QR stickers are printed on weatherproof, UV-resistant vinyl rated for outdoor use. They are tested for Indian climate conditions — monsoon humidity, direct sun exposure, and temperature extremes. The adhesive is automotive-grade. Once applied, the sticker will not peel, fade, or become unscannable under normal conditions.

### How does call masking work when someone scans my sticker?
The caller taps the contact button on your vehicle profile. The call routes through ProfileTap's masking system. They hear it ring, you answer on your real phone, and you have a normal conversation. At no point does either party see the other's personal mobile number. Once the call ends, there is no record of your actual number on the caller's phone.

### Can I use one sticker for both my car and my bike?
Each vehicle needs its own sticker because each sticker links to a specific vehicle profile. However, you manage all your vehicle profiles from a single ProfileTap account. Create a profile for your car, another for your bike, order the appropriate sticker variant for each, and manage everything from one dashboard.

### What if I sell my vehicle — can I transfer the sticker to a new owner?
You can deactivate the profile linked to a sticker at any time. If you sell the vehicle, deactivate the profile so the QR code no longer displays your information. The new owner can create their own ProfileTap profile and order a new sticker. Alternatively, if you buy a new vehicle, you order a new sticker for it and create a fresh profile — your old vehicle's profile can be archived or deleted.

## How PVC NFC Business Cards Work

ProfileTap PVC business cards use the same technology as the metal range. An embedded NFC chip and a printed QR code connect the physical card to your ProfileTap digital profile. When someone taps the card on their smartphone or scans the QR code, your profile opens in their browser — no app needed.

Your digital profile is fully editable. Change your phone number, update your designation, add a new portfolio link — the card never goes out of date. This is the core advantage over traditional paper visiting cards: print once, update forever.

PVC cards are thicker and more durable than paper but lighter and more flexible than metal. They fit comfortably in a wallet or cardholder and withstand everyday handling without cracking or fading.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How are PVC cards different from the metal cards?

The technology is identical — both have NFC chips and QR codes linked to your ProfileTap digital profile. The difference is material and feel. Metal cards are heavier, more rigid, and have a luxury finish. PVC cards are lighter, slightly flexible, and more affordable. Both do the same job. Choose based on your budget and the impression you want to make.

### Are PVC business cards durable?

Yes. ProfileTap PVC cards are made from high-quality PVC plastic — the same material used in bank cards and ID cards. They resist water, bending, and everyday wear. The NFC chip is sealed inside and rated for years of regular use. They will long outlast any paper visiting card.

### Can I order PVC cards for my entire team?

Absolutely. PVC cards are the most popular choice for team orders because of the lower per-unit cost. Each team member gets their own card linked to their individual ProfileTap profile. A manager can oversee all profiles and analytics from a single dashboard. Bulk pricing is available for orders of 10 or more cards.

### Can I switch from a PVC card to a metal card later?

Yes. Your ProfileTap digital profile is independent of the physical card. If you start with a PVC card and later decide to upgrade to a metal card, your profile, analytics history, and all your data carry over. Just link the new card to your existing profile.

## What Is ProfileTap Business AI?

ProfileTap Business AI is a suite of AI-powered tools built directly into your [ProfileTap business identity platform](/business-identity). It is not a separate product or a third-party integration — it lives inside the same profile you already use for NFC sharing, QR codes, and client management.

**What it includes:**

- **AI Review Assistant** — Automatically draft professional review responses, request reviews at the right moment, and monitor your reputation across platforms
- **Smart Follow-Up Suggestions** — AI analyses profile views and engagement data to suggest when and how to follow up with prospects
- **Client Engagement Insights** — Go beyond basic analytics to understand which contacts are warm, which went cold, and what actions drive conversions
- **Automated Business Summaries** — Weekly AI-generated reports on profile performance, review trends, and engagement patterns

## How Business AI Works With Your ProfileTap Profile

Business AI is not a bolt-on. It reads from the same data your profile already generates — every scan, every view, every review, every shared contact.

### Step 1: Your Profile Generates Data

Every time someone taps your NFC card, scans your QR code, or views your digital profile, ProfileTap captures engagement data. Every Google review you receive through your profile link is tracked.

### Step 2: Business AI Analyses Patterns

The AI layer processes this data to find patterns you would miss manually:
- A prospect viewed your profile three times this week but has not called
- Your response rate to Google reviews dropped last month
- Your Tuesday post-meeting shares convert better than Friday cold shares

### Step 3: You Get Actionable Suggestions

Business AI surfaces these insights as clear, actionable items:
- "Follow up with Rajesh Mehta — 3 profile views in 5 days, no contact yet"
- "You have 4 Google reviews awaiting response — here are AI-drafted replies"
- "Your profile engagement is 40% higher when you include your portfolio link"

### Step 4: You Act, AI Learns

As you act on suggestions — sending follow-ups, responding to reviews, updating your profile — Business AI refines its recommendations. It gets smarter the more you use it.

## Key Business AI Features

### AI Review Management

Reviews drive local business in India. A 4.5-star Google rating is the difference between a packed waiting room and an empty one. Business AI helps you:

- **Draft professional responses** to both positive and negative reviews in seconds
- **Request reviews** at the optimal moment — right after a successful interaction, not three weeks later
- **Monitor review sentiment** trends over time so you catch problems early
- **Generate QR-based review request cards** that make leaving a review effortless for your customers

This builds on ProfileTap's existing [AI review assist](/ai-review-assist) and [Google review management](/google-review-management) tools, adding predictive intelligence on top.

### Smart Engagement Analytics

Standard analytics tell you how many people viewed your profile. Business AI tells you what to do about it:

| Standard Analytics | Business AI Analytics |
|---|---|
| 47 profile views this week | 12 of those viewers came back twice — likely warm leads |
| 8 QR scans at the expo | 3 scans came from decision-makers at target companies |
| 15 new contacts saved | 6 contacts have not been followed up within 48 hours |

### Automated Weekly Reports

Every Monday, Business AI sends you a summary:
- Total profile engagement vs previous week
- Review activity and sentiment
- Follow-up recommendations
- Top-performing sharing method (NFC vs QR vs link)

No dashboard to check. No numbers to crunch. Just a clear summary of what happened and what to do next.

### Team Intelligence (for Multi-Account Users)

Businesses using [multi-account team management](/business-identity) can see aggregated Business AI insights across all team members:
- Which team member's profile drives the most engagement
- Team-wide review response rates
- Collective follow-up pipeline

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is ProfileTap Business AI?

ProfileTap Business AI is a set of AI-powered tools built into your ProfileTap business profile. It includes AI review management, smart follow-up suggestions, engagement analytics, and automated weekly business reports — all designed for Indian SMBs, freelancers, and teams.

### Does Business AI cost extra?

Business AI features are included with ProfileTap business profiles. Some advanced features like team intelligence may require a premium plan. Check the pricing page for current details.

### How is this different from using ChatGPT for my business?

ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI with no access to your business data. ProfileTap Business AI is connected to your actual profile engagement — it sees who viewed your profile, which reviews need responses, and which follow-ups are overdue. It gives specific, actionable recommendations, not generic advice.

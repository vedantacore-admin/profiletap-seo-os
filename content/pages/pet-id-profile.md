Traditional pet ID tags have a fundamental flaw: they are fixed. Engrave your phone number on a metal tag and the day you change your number, that tag becomes useless. ProfileTap pet profiles are different. Your pet's QR tag links to a live digital profile you can update anytime — and every scan always shows the latest information.

## Why Smart Pet ID Tags Matter

Every pet owner's nightmare is a lost pet. In Indian cities — where open gates, festival crowds, and Diwali fireworks can send even the calmest dog into a panic — it happens more often than anyone would like. When it does, a traditional tag with a scratched-off number or an old address is not enough.

A ProfileTap pet profile works differently. The QR code on your pet's tag never changes — it always points to the same online profile. But the profile itself? You update it whenever you need to. New phone number, new address, new vet — change it in seconds and every future scan reflects the update instantly. Anyone who finds your pet scans the tag with their phone camera and sees everything they need to get your companion home safely.

## How Pet QR Profiles Work

The process is simple for everyone involved — including the stranger who finds your pet.

**Step 1 — Scan the QR tag**
Someone finds your dog or cat. They use their phone camera to scan the QR code on the tag. No app needed. Any modern smartphone camera works.

**Step 2 — See the pet's profile**
A clean profile page opens immediately. It shows your pet's name, photo, owner contact details, vet information, and any medical notes. The finder knows exactly who this pet belongs to and how to reach you.

**Step 3 — Contact the owner**
The finder calls or messages you directly from the profile page. You receive the call on your phone, your pet is returned, and the story has a happy ending.

You control exactly what is visible. Add as much or as little as you feel comfortable sharing.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does a pet QR tag work?**
A pet QR tag contains a QR code that links to your pet's ProfileTap profile. Anyone with a smartphone camera can scan it — no app needed. The profile opens immediately and shows your pet's information and your contact details. You create and manage the profile online, and updates are reflected instantly on every future scan.

**What if the QR code gets scratched or worn?**
QR codes can tolerate some damage and still scan correctly — they include error correction built into the encoding. However, if a tag is heavily worn, you can simply print a new QR code (the URL never changes) and attach it. For pets that are rough on their tags, consider laminating the QR code or using a waterproof tag material.

**Can I update the profile after I have already printed and attached the tag?**
Yes — that is one of the core advantages of a QR pet profile. The QR code on the tag always links to the same profile URL. You update the profile online, and the next time someone scans the tag, they see the updated information. You never need to reprint the tag just because your phone number changed.

**Does it work for cats as well as dogs?**
Absolutely. ProfileTap pet profiles work for any pet you want to protect. Cats, dogs, rabbits, birds — if you can attach a QR tag to them (or to their carrier or cage), a ProfileTap profile gives whoever finds them the information they need to reach you.

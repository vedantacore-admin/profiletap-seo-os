## What is Business Identity Management?

Business identity management is the practice of controlling how you and your business are presented across every touchpoint — in-person meetings, online searches, customer reviews, and team interactions. A well-managed business identity builds trust faster and converts more prospects into clients.

Traditional tools — paper cards, PDF portfolios, a basic Google My Business listing — are static. They go out of date, they break, and they give you no insight into what is working. ProfileTap replaces that stack with a single, living platform that connects your online and offline identity.

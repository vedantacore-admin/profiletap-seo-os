**What is ProfileTap?**

Profiles can be shared via NFC tap, QR code scan, or a direct link. It is not just a digital business card — it is a platform that manages every aspect of your identity.

**Is ProfileTap free?**
ProfileTap offers a free plan that includes one digital profile and basic features. Paid plans unlock additional profiles, advanced analytics, team collaboration, AI-powered review tools, and call and WhatsApp privacy. See the [pricing page](/pricing) for full details.

**What is the difference between NFC sharing and QR sharing?**
NFC sharing requires an NFC-enabled ProfileTap card or tag. When you tap it on any modern smartphone, your profile opens automatically — no camera, no scanning needed. QR sharing works with any camera app. You print or display your QR code and anyone can scan it. Both methods open the same live profile.

**Who is ProfileTap for?**
ProfileTap serves a wide range of users: business professionals, freelancers, sales teams, doctors, real estate agents, social media creators, parents, pet owners, travelers, and vehicle owners. The platform is India-first but works for users anywhere in the world.

## Why Indian Professionals Are Moving Away from Paper Cards

Paper business cards have been the default for decades. But the problems are well understood by anyone who has attended a business event in Pune or a client meeting in Gurugram.

**They go out of date fast.** A new mobile number, a new office address, a new job title — and the 500 cards you printed three months ago are wrong. You either distribute incorrect information or spend money reprinting.

**They get lost.** A card exchanged at a trade show in Chennai has roughly a 30% chance of surviving the week in the recipient's wallet. Most end up forgotten or discarded.

**They give you nothing back.** A paper card cannot tell you whether the person who received it ever looked at your details, clicked your website, or saved your number.

**They are wasteful.** Indian professionals collectively print hundreds of millions of paper cards every year. Every version update means more waste.

A digital business card solves all four problems. Your profile is always current, always accessible, and every interaction with it is tracked.
